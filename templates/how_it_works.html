How SatyaVani Works कसरी काम गर्छ

A secure, transparent, and anonymous platform for exposing corruption in Nepal

01

Complete Anonymity

We use cryptographic tokens instead of accounts. No email, no name, no phone number is ever collected. Our servers do not log IP addresses. You can use Tor Browser for maximum anonymity.

02

Evidence Submission

Upload documents (DOCX, PDF, Excel), images (JPG, PNG), videos (MP4, AVI), audio recordings (MP3, WAV), and archive files. We automatically strip metadata from uploaded files to protect your identity.

03

Your Tracking Token

After submission, you receive a unique 43-character cryptographic token. This token allows you to check your report's status anonymously. Store it safely — it cannot be recovered.

04

Review & Verification

Our team of investigators reviews each submission within 24-48 hours. Reports are assessed for credibility, cross-referenced with other cases, and categorized for action.

05

Escalation to Authorities

Verified and credible reports are escalated to appropriate authorities including CIAA Nepal, National Vigilance Centre, Nepal Police Anti-Corruption Bureau, and international bodies.

06

Publication

Confirmed cases may be published publicly (with your consent, implied through submission) to increase public pressure and awareness around systemic corruption.
